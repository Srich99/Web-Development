Maggie Receipe

It is a noodle type receipe.It can be prepared very easily.Not much ingriedients are needed.It can be cooked in 5 mins.It is very tasty.it can be a snack item or breakfast too.

  • water
  • maggie
  • masala
  • carrots
  • beans
  1. Take some water in a pan 🚰
  2. Add maggie to water 🍳
    • break maggie into pieces.🍽️
  3. Adding vegetables to maggie🍅🍅
    • Chop carrots
    • Chop onions
    • Add both of them to maggie
  4. Add masala to it🌶️🌶️
  5. leave it for 5 mins

Your Maggie is Ready:)🍲🍲🍲
